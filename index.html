Complex Social Structure

Honeybees live in highly organized colonies with a complex social structure. Each colony consists of a queen, worker bees, and drones. The queen is responsible for reproduction, while worker bees perform various tasks such as foraging, nursing, and defending the hive. Drones' primary role is to mate with the queen.
